Our Mission

AI is moving fast. The gap between people who understand it and people who don't is growing wider every month — and that gap determines who benefits from the technology and who gets left behind. Our mission is to close that gap.

We believe good AI education shouldn't require a computer science degree, an expensive course, or hours of sifting through contradictory blog posts. It should be accurate, honest about limitations, and written like a knowledgeable friend explains it to you — not a textbook, not a sales pitch.

What We Are Not

We are not affiliated with, sponsored by, or paid by Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Meta, or any other AI company. We do not receive referral fees for recommending any product or service. All opinions and comparisons are independent.

AI information changes rapidly. While we aim to keep everything accurate and up to date, we strongly recommend verifying key facts — especially pricing, context windows, and benchmark scores — against official documentation, as these change frequently.

Who Built This?

AI Literate was built by practitioners who work with large language models and AI systems daily. The content draws on hands-on experience with the tools described, not just documentation summaries. We know which parts confuse people because we've been confused by them too.
